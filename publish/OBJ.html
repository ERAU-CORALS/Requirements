1.0 Simulation Control Algorithm OBJ-1

CORALS should utilize a control algorithm to successfully demonstrate yaw, pitch, and roll control in a computer simulation.

2.0 Wireless Telemetry OBJ-2

CORALS should transmit data wirelessly to continuously stream telemetry data throughout CORALS' operation.

3.0 Range of Motion OBJ-3

CORALS should be capable of controlled movement in yaw to ±180 degrees of motion, and in pitch and roll to ±45 degrees of motion.

4.0 Roof Arrangement OBJ-4

CORALS should utilize a Control Moment Gyroscope array with a lower likelihood of gimbal lock by using a roof arrangement.

5.0 Pivot Endurance OBJ-5

CORALS should utilize a pivot point that is durable for a duration longer than a week of continuous use.
